The chess man and his daughter

The Moral

You should never give up on your dreams

Once, there was a father who used to play chess every evening

This man had a daughter who was 10 years old

When she turned 11, she asked her father to teach her play chess

The man agreed and started telling her the rules

The girl seemed to enjoy it so she learned everything pretty quickly

When she prepared herself for the first game, she asked her father to join and practice with her

Her father was pretty proud and so they started round 1.

The game was though and her father would always win.

But the girl never gave up.
